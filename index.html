Hyperlink

Hyperlink is a personal search engine to that unveils the hidden Internet for oneself. It integrates various data sources like social networks, cloud storage and web collection. A unified entry-point to search one's personal channels is provided. Besides our web-based service, we also provide a browser extension to enhance traditional search engine results with relevant findings from Hyperlink.
